# Mechanical Engineering Projects
**Varun Suresh | Michigan State University | Mechanical Engineering**

A collection of CAD and mechanical engineering projects demonstrating 
proficiency in Siemens NX, SolidWorks, and Autodesk Fusion 360.

## Projects

### 🔧 Automotive Turbocharger CAD Assembly
**Software:** Siemens NX  
**Timeline:** February 2026 – Present  
Designed a multi-stage turbocharger to study the mechanical integration 
of high-speed turbine and compressor systems. Applied advanced assembly 
constraints, GD&T callouts, ASME documentation standards, and DFM review.

---

### 🔧 Radial Engine CAD Assembly
**Software:** SolidWorks  
**Timeline:** December 2025 – February 2026  
Modeled a 5-cylinder radial engine to study mechanical timing of the 
main and articulated rod system. Ran motion studies to verify kinematics 
and clearances, performed FEA stress analysis, and generated a structured BOM.
